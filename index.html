Intro

Hi there! I'm Connie. I'm a full stack software engineer based in NYC.

My first experience with code was building website templates with simple HTML and CSS when I was 12 to keep myself busy during the summer. At the time, I didn't know what programming was.

I found excitement in Math so naturally it became what I studied in college, but had subsequently planned on pursuing a second bachelor's degree in Nursing.

During my experience as a nursing assistant, while exploring the features of the medical software I was required to learn, my curiosity in tech resurfaced. From there, I made the decision to dive into the world of software development. Recently, I've completed an immersive software engineering bootcamp at The Grace Hopper Program at Fullstack Academy.

When I'm not coding, I'm either baking, trying new food or visiting aquariums.
